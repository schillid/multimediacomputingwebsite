Most recent: Progress on the Subaru

I started working on the side of the car, back light and spoiler. I modelled after some reference pictures I found on Google Images.

Here I was confronted with a slight problem, I had to connect multiple edges coming from the top of the trunk to only two on the back but as you can see, I was able to connect them with quads only. On the right you can see the Turbosmooth isoline display preview.

Here you can see the finished side of the car. I also applied symmetry to the model, to see if everything is scaled properly.

I then created some floating geometry for the horizontal indentation in the door to create a nice normal map later and started working on the roof and windows.
